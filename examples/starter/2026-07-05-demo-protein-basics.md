Read up on daily protein: the common evidence-backed range for active people
is roughly 1.6 to 2.2 g per kg of body weight, spread across meals. The
interesting part was that more is not better past that range — the body just
oxidizes the surplus. Notes like this one show the brain is not only for
computer science.
