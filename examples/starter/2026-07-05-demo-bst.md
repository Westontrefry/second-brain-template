Implemented insert and search on a BST: smaller keys go left, larger go right,
so search halves the space each step when the tree is balanced. Both fell out
naturally as recursive functions. Unbalanced trees degrade to a linked list —
that is apparently what AVL and red-black trees fix, parked for later.
